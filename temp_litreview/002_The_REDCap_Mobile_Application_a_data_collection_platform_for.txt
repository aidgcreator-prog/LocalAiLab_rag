Title: The REDCap Mobile Application: a data collection platform for research in regions or situations with internet scarcity
Authors: Paul A. Harris; Giovanni Delacqua; Robert J. Taylor; Scott Pearson; Michelle Fernández; Stephany N. Duda
Year: 2021
Journal: JAMIA Open
DOI: 10.1093/jamiaopen/ooab078
Citations: 75

Abstract:
Guidance recommendations based on designing, developing, deploying, and disseminating the REDCap Mobile Application may help other teams looking to develop clinical research informatics applications.
